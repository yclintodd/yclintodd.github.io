關於我們｜About Us

本事務所由專業環境工程技師創立，具備製造業環境管理與數位轉型多年經驗，專注於環境資料分析、感測技術、AI應用與污染控制設計。

Our office is founded by a licensed professional environmental engineer with extensive experience in manufacturing environmental management and digital transformation. We specialize in environmental data analysis, sensing technologies, AI applications, and pollution control system design.
